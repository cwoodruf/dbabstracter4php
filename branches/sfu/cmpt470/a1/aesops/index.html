The text of each fable is enhanced with tools to help English learners understand the text. Highlighted words in the text show pictures and illustrations are annotated with tips to help the reader understand the gist of the story.

Html text and pictures from Project Gutenberg. Word pictures are from the Photographic Dictionary. Picture of Aesop on this page is from Wikipedia.
